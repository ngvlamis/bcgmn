About BCGMN

The Backgammon Club of the Great Manhattan North is a competitive backgammon club in the Washington Heights neighborhood of Manhattan in New York City. We welcome players of all levels who share a love of the game.

Where & When

Le Chéile — 839 W 181st St, Washington Heights, Manhattan
A train to 181st St

We meet on the second floor of the restaurant.

Registration 7:00 pm  ·  Play begins 7:30 pm

House Rules

Each meeting is a 3-round Swiss tournament — everyone plays all three rounds regardless of wins or losses, with pairings based on similar records. A bye is assigned when there's an odd number of players. We play under USBGF rules with the following specifics and exceptions:

  • 7-point matches
  • Clocks are required — 12:30 per player with a 12-second delay per move
  • Dice landing on checkers are valid and must be played, provided the checkers are flat (no finger dishes); otherwise the dice must be re-rolled
  • We use the Legal Moves policy — illegal checker moves must be corrected if noticed before the opponent has made a valid roll; once the opponent rolls, the move stands. Both players are obligated to point out illegal moves; failing to do so is tantamount to cheating.

BMAB

BCGMN tournaments are valid BMAB entries. The Backgammon Masters Awarding Body awards mastership titles to players based on their performance rating (PR) across verified tournament matches — submitting your results is how you earn titles like Master and Grandmaster.

How Standings Work

Points are awarded per meeting and standings reflect the current calendar year only.

Entry & Prizes

There is a $20 buy-in per meeting, plus an optional $10 sidepool. Of the $20: $10 goes to the night's pool, $5 goes to the end-of-year pool (awarded to the top player in the annual standings), and $5 goes to the venue.

The night's pool is split equally among all players who finish 3–0. The sidepool is split equally among sidepool participants with the best record on the night.
